CODING & DEBUGGING

EVENT DETAILS

GENERAL GUIDELINES:

• The event is for individual participants.
• During the quiz participants should keep their camera switched on, throughout the zoom session. If any participant’s video is switched off for a longer period it will led to immediate elimination.
• The event consists of two rounds preliminary and final round.
• The participants should have a Laptop/Desktop with webcam and good internet connection.
• Participants should have a laptop/desktop for participating.
• Quiz link and Zoom link will be sent to the registered participants. Late joining will not be entertained.

ROUND 1: Aptitude Round

Rules:
• Round will be based on basic programming concept of c and java
• The round consists of 30 MCQ questions,1 point for each correct answers and no negative marking.
• The time constraint is 20 minutes.
• The qualifying participants will proceed to the Next round.
• Top 7 will be selected for final round. In case of tie, submission time will be considered.

FINAL ROUND

Round 1 (Coding)

• Round will be based on basic programming concept of c and java
• The round consists of 4 questions, 2 from C and 2 from java.
• The time constraint is 20 minutes.
• There will be no negative points.
• The qualifying participants will proceed to the Next round.
• Top 4 will be selected for final round. In case of tie, submission time will be considered

Round 2 (Debugging)

• Round will be based on basic programming concept of c and java
• The round consists of 3 questions, 2 from C and 1 from java.
• The time constraint is 15 minutes.
• There will be no negative points.
• Winners will be decided from this round.
• In case of tie, submission time and points of round 1 will be considered.
