EVENT DETAILS

GENERAL GUIDELINES:

• The event is for individual participants.
• During the quiz participants should keep their camera switched on, throughout the zoom session. If any participant’s video is switched off for a longer period it will led to immediate elimination.
• The event consists of two rounds preliminary and final round.
• The participants should have a Laptop/Desktop with webcam and good internet connection.
• Participants should have a laptop/desktop for participating.
• Quiz link and Zoom link will be sent to the registered participants. Late joining will not be entertained.
• The event is timebound and consists of two rounds.(preliminary, final)
• The event will be based on testing basic knowledge in the field of physics which comprises of logical reasoning, understanding of the subject and problem solving.
• The overall score of final round will be taken into account to decide the final winner.
